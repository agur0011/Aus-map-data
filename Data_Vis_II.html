Housing Conditions and Mobility

Exploring housing repairs, mobility, and living satisfaction in Australia

Housing Repairs by State

This heatmap shows various repairs and the percentage of homes that require each type of repair. It can help inform decision-making regarding what to inspect in a home within each state.

Resident Mobility by State

Please interact with the legend to select a duration for years of stay. This allows comparison between states with more short-term and long-term residents, aiding in decisions about relocating for work or family reasons.

Housing Satisfaction by Dwelling Type

Housing satisfaction is an important metric. The highest satisfaction is with semi-detached/row or terrace house/townhouses at 41.3%. This metric can inform decisions on property types less prone to damage and inconvenience.

Rental Agreement Types by State

12-month rental agreements are the most common, especially in Tasmania, representing 61.8% of rental agreements. Short-term rentals are prevalent in the Northern Territory (18.5%) and Queensland (16.5%), which could guide those seeking temporary housing.

House Deposit Requirements by State

In Western Australia, 45.9% of houses are bought with a deposit under 10%, making it favorable for young buyers. This chart helps prospective buyers understand state-specific deposit requirements.
