Housing conditions and Mobility

This choropleth map shows various repairs and the percentage of homes which are reported to require each type of repair. It can help inform decision making regarding what to inspect in a home within each state.

Please interact with the legend to select a duration for years of stay. This way it is obvious which to compare which states have more residents in shorter term or longer term living situations. This can be useful to make a decision regarding moving for either work or family reasons.
